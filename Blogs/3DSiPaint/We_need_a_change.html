Admins, you don't have to delete this blog


A Letter

Have you seen the current state of the site? Dead. Have you seen Drac's blog showing what admin is active and who not? In a nutshell: most of the admins are inactive. Nobody is able to approve and decline new accounts. No new people can get in. The activity levels will fall and fall and fall and fall-

What can we do about it?
Nothing. Genuinely nothing. Unless a wonder happens and someone gets overlord to actually do stuff, we're just going to be stuck in an infinite loop of the same members and decreasing activity. We might be able to make events, but no new members can come in, remember?

So what we need is an admin purge and afterwards an election.

Most people I know were alright with the idea of inactive admins getting demoted. Most of the admins said that there were too many admins at the moment for an election. If we first remove, we can add. Simple. Majority wins anyway.

Now obviously I can't do this myself as I'm just a helpdeskmin. So this is essentially a letter for you will. If you agree with all I said, comment in the following format:

username - member since (date of joining)

If we have enough votes, we might be able to make the change needed. If you have any questions, DM me. Thanks for your time. May we be an active community once again. -Redwood
